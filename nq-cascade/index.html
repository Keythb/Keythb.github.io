The 1h→2h gate is the ladder's bottleneck at 62% (n=72). Forward rungs above a fulcrum compound the conditional odds into a cumulative ~% with a reach-by deadline. Payoff is regime-dependent (finding 6): position golden in a bull regime averages +23.96%, while position death in a bear regime averages -3.19% — a trap, not a signal. RSI turns green when it agrees with the cross (≥60 golden / ≤40 death — trend runs ~1.95x longer, finding 2) and red when it disagrees.
Gate logic: a cascade starts at the most recent 5m cross and advances chart by chart. Only the frontier (the highest chart reached) matters — the cascade stays alive while the frontier holds its direction, dies if the frontier flips back before the next chart crosses (or the gate's measured p90 window closes), and completes at the 4H; a completed cascade then watches the 1D wall until the daily aligns. Lower charts flipping back does not break it; charts already in the move's direction count as aligned on arrival. Measured gate odds are shown on the open gate.

Gate logic: a cascade starts at the most recent 5m cross and advances chart by chart. Only the frontier (the highest chart reached) matters — the cascade stays alive while the frontier holds its direction, dies if the frontier flips back before the next chart crosses (or the gate's measured p90 window closes), and completes at the 4H; a completed cascade then watches the 1D wall until the daily aligns. Lower charts flipping back does not break it; charts already in the move's direction count as aligned on arrival. Measured gate odds are shown on the open gate.
